Fabio Pastore
Founder & CEO

My name is Fabio Pastore and i’m a last year computer engineer student.


I strongly believe that a business is the reflection of the owner's personality and way of thinking, so with engineering critical thinking, and managing skills, joining my ability to read people's needs i decided to found BINDS MEDIA, to meet the needs of the e-commerce and startup owners.


My goal and job as a Founder is to be always available for the clients and make sure they are happy and are receiving our high-quality service. That’s why I delegate the actual service delivery to a professional media buyer (who you will know and interact with), so I can fulfill your requests and be always there to resolve your problems.


I’ve selected an experienced media buyer after months and months of research because I want to guarantee a certain level of professionalism, something that in this Advertisement world is often forgotten.


I base my relationship with clients on transparency, making sure everything is clear and I don’t want to leave any doubt, especially because I work with brand's images and credibility.


If you are in doubt about whether to jump on board, just book a call with me and I’ll be happy to have a talk with you and clear every doubt you have.
